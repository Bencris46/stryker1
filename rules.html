These are the rules that every single member must follow, no matter who you are or what rank/role you have.

1.) Discord ToS

Discord has their own rules that all Discord users must follow, if you break the ToS, you will be banned and reported. Discord Terms of Services: https://discordapp.com/terms



2.) Chatting/Conversation

- No spamming or flooding text, links, images, emojis, files, or any other form of messages.
- No discussion/mention of NSFW or gore related topics
- No disturbing images/emojis within chat
- No unknown links
- Do not post any illegal content. This includes the discussion of illegal content.
- Do not talk about religious or political topics



3.) Behavior

- Respect everyone, no matter who they are
- Do not harass or be toxic to any other users within the server
- Do not be racist, sexist, homophobic, ant-religious, etc.



4.) Appearance

- You must have an appropiate Profile Picture, Profile Banner, About Me, and Username. No NSFW.
- No Impersonation of any bot or people within the server



5.) Privacy & Safety

- No sharing any personal information (Real Name, Address, Pictures, etc.) of any other user, even if they let you. Only YOU can share your own information
- No doxxing



6.) Other

- Don't minimod. If there's a issue, ping a active staff member
- Don't beg for nitro or anything else within the server
- No Advertising (In the server and in DMs)
